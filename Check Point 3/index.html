Recommended Diet for Athletes

Pre-Workout Meal (For Energy & Endurance)

  • Whole-grain bread with peanut butter
  • Oatmeal with fruits
  • Banana with yogurt
  • Brown rice with grilled chicken

Post-Workout Meal (For Recovery & Muscle Repair)

  • Protein shake with nuts
  • Grilled salmon with quinoa
  • Scrambled eggs with avocado toast
  • Greek yogurt with honey and seeds

Hydration & Snacks (For Sustained Performance)

  • Water and electrolyte drinks
  • Nuts and dried fruits
  • Fresh fruit smoothies
  • Dark chocolate for energy boost

Essential Nutrients (For Strength & Stamina)

  • Protein: Lean meat, fish, eggs, legumes
  • Healthy Fats: Avocados, nuts, olive oil
  • Vitamins & Minerals: Green vegetables, fruits, dairy
